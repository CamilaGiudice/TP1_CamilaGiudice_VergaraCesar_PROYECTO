Pistachio Sourdough

Ingredients

Name Quantity
Gluten Free Bread Flour360g
Active Sourdough Starter60g
Psyllium husk5g
Salt10g
Baking Powder2g
Warm Water390ml
Olive Oil26ml
Honey21g
Baking Soda2g

Procedure

1) Feed the starter with 50g of gluten free bread flour and 50ml of warm water. Let it rest til double size.
2) In a bowl, mix the liquids with the psyllium husk and let it rest until it forms a gel.
3) Mix the dry ingredients with the liquids and form the dough.
4) Knead the dough and let it sit at room temperature to double it´s size (6-8 hours).
5) Preheat the oven at 250°C.
6) Bake the shaped bread for 30 minutes.
7) Drop the temperature to 200°C and finish baking for another 20 minutes.
8) Remove from the oven and let it cool before cutting.
